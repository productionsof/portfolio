Navigate app

Navigate student app

Product strategy, product design

At the edtech startup where I worked, we needed to help students narrow down their degree major choices in an efficient and satisfying way. Over 40% of students change their majors at least once. If we could lower this number, we could help students feel better about their degree when complete and also avoid wasting time and money on courses that would no longer relate.

A product existed in the company, but it was very limited. The new product I was tasked to create needed to solve all of these problems.

The goal

Design a user-centric degree major selection tool that helps students grow in understanding their life purpose and their confidence in a degree selection.

The process

  • Interviewed stakeholders
  • Conducted content audit
  • Identified key metrics
  • Conducted research
  • Outlined information architecture
  • Designed content flow
  • Created content
  • Tested content
  • Conducted beta program
  • Created training program for staff
  • Launched MVP
