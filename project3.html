IBM Systems Cost Estimator

User research, UX design, product strategy

IBM lacked a pricing tool that could show site visitors and potential clients a real-time hardware price. Although it was one of the most requested features in our user reserach, iIBM had never been able to successfully deliver due to complex back-end systems.

The goal

Create a real-time hardware pricing configurator that allows users to see current pricing for strategic hardware offerings.

The process

  • Conducted initial kickoff
  • Audited competitor tools
  • Conducted generative user tests to gain insight into their desires and expectations
  • Created low-fidelity wireframes
  • Conducted rapid testing with users to eliminate several possible directions
  • Worked with developers and business stakeholders to build the tool
  • Launched MVP
